## Support and Feedback

If you have any questions or feedback, please reach out to us via our [Support Telegram]() or [Discord (Support Channel)](). We normally respond within 24 hours. We would love to hear your thoughts!

## Contributing

If you want to contribute to Area, please check out our [GitHub repositories](https://github.com/EigenExplorer). We welcome contributions of all kinds, including bug fixes, feature requests, and documentation improvements.

## Thank You

We'd like to thank everyone who has supported, provided feedback, and contributed to Area. Your support is greatly appreciated.
